Visual Guide: Daily Temperatures

The "Aha!" Moment: Store the indices of colder days in a Stack. When a warm day arrives, pop all the stored indices that are colder than the new arrival. The wait time is simply: new_index - popped_index

Tracing: [75, 71, 69, 72]

Step 1: Push Unmatched Temps

Idx 0 (75), 1 (71), 2 (69)

None of them are warmer than the person below them on the stack. They all just sit down.

Res 0 0 0 0
[69, i=2]
[71, i=1]
[75, i=0]

Step 2: The Warm Day Arrives!

Idx 3 (72) arrives.

First match: 72 > 69. POP 69.
Wait time: 3 - 2 = 1.

Second match: 72 > 71. POP 71.
Wait time: 3 - 1 = 2.

Third match: 72 is NOT > 75. Stop popping.

Res 0 2 1 0
← Popped 69
← Popped 71
[72, i=3]
[75, i=0]
