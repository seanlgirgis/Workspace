Visual Guide: Car Fleet

The "Aha!" Moment: Process cars from the target backwards. If my theoretical arrival time is LESS THAN the car in front of me, I will inevitably crash into it. We merge into one fleet, and I adopt the slower arrival time of the car blocking me.

Calculations (Processed Right to Left)

1. Pos 10: (12 - 10) / 2 = 1.0 hours.
(No cars ahead. It becomes Fleet 1, arriving at T=1.0)

2. Pos 8: (12 - 8) / 4 = 1.0 hours.
Does 1.0 ≤ 1.0 (Fleet 1 time)? Yes. Car 8 crashes into Car 10! Merges into Fleet 1

3. Pos 5: (12 - 5) / 1 = 7.0 hours.
Does 7.0 ≤ 1.0 (Fleet 1 time)? No. It's too slow. Forms a new fleet. Fleet 2 Formed (Time: 7.0)

4. Pos 3: (12 - 3) / 3 = 3.0 hours.
Does 3.0 ≤ 7.0 (Fleet 2 time)? Yes! It's fast enough to crash into Car 5! Merges into Fleet 2

5. Pos 0: (12 - 0) / 1 = 12.0 hours.
Does 12.0 ≤ 7.0 (Fleet 2 time)? No. Too slow. Forms new fleet. Fleet 3 Formed

Total Fleets Arriving: 3
