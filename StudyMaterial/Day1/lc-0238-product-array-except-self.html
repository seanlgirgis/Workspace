Visual Guide: Product of Array Except Self

Array: [1, 2, 3, 4]

The "Aha!" Moment: For any item `i`, the answer is the product of everything to its left, multiplied by everything to its right.
Answer[i] = Prefix[i] * Postfix[i]. We can construct these in two sweeps.

Step 1: Prefix Sweep (Left to Right)

What is the product of everything to the left of me? (Initialize with 1).

Original Nums 1 2 3 4
Prefix Array 1
(Nothing left)		 1
(1)		 2
(1*2)		 6
(1*2*3)

Step 2: Postfix Sweep (Right to Left)

What is the product of everything to the right of me? (Multiply into the prefix array).

Original Nums 1 2 3 4
Postfix Value 24
(2*3*4)		 12
(3*4)		 4
(4)		 1
(Nothing right)

Result (Prefix * Postfix)

Final Answer 24
(1 * 24)		 12
(1 * 12)		 8
(2 * 4)		 6
(6 * 1)
