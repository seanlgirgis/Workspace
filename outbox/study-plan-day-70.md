# Day 70 — Final Confidence Assessment + Readiness Verdict

**Theme:** Ten weeks done. This is the moment of honest reckoning. Not self-doubt — clear-eyed assessment. What are you ready to close? What still needs work?

---

## Daily Maintenance (20 min)

**LC — Consolidation (2 problems, your two weakest patterns):**
Identify the 2 patterns you feel least confident in right now. Do 1 problem from each. 10 min each.

**SQL — Explain a complex query:**
Pick any query from Days 48, 69. Read it. Then close your notes and explain each CTE, line by line, out loud. Can you explain what the business is asking for, why each join exists, and what the window function is tracking?

## Week 11 Planning — Offer Stage (10 min)

Week 11 theme: closing. If you're in final rounds, Week 11 is about staying sharp while navigating the offer process — not grinding new content.

**Week 11 focus:**
- Day 71: Offer evaluation framework (total comp, role scope, team quality)
- Day 72: Negotiation execution (counter-offer, silence as a tool)
- Day 73: Managing multiple offers (exploding offers, timeline compression)
- Day 74: Reference check prep
- Day 75: Decision framework (if you have to choose)
- Day 76: Verbal acceptance + next steps
- Day 77: Week 11 close + transition mindset shift
