## What Comes Next

The 91-day plan ends here, but the habits don't have to.

**Keep doing:**
- Monthly: 15 minutes of network maintenance
- Quarterly: behavioral story review — update with new experiences
- Annually: assess your technical trajectory — are you growing? Are you still excited?

**In the new role:**
- First 30 days: listen more than you talk
- First 60 days: one visible contribution
- First 90 days: own something

**When the next search comes** (and it will, eventually):
Come back to Day 1. The plan will still work. The patterns will still apply. You'll just ramp faster — because you've done this before.
